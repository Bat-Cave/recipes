## Directions

1. Combine **first 5 ingredients** into a mixing bowl.
2. Add <ReactiveIngredient slug="basic-bread" ingredientIndex={5} servings={5} quantity={2} /> and spin breifly.
3. Let sit for **10 minutes**.
4. Start mixing again while adding the remaining flour, <ReactiveIngredient slug="basic-bread" ingredientIndex={5} servings={5} quantity={2} omitName /> at a time.
5. Add flour until you can see the sides pull clean, as well as the dough coming clean from the bottom of the bowl.
6. The dough should be very sticky and does not come together at this point, but the bowl is clean.
7. Knead the dough for **10 minutes** or until the gluten has been developed. If the dough starts sticking, simply add a little white flour to take up the moisture.

8. Cover the mixer with a towel and let the dough rise for **20-30 minutes** in mixer.
9. Uncover dough, oil hands and counter, and remove dough from the mixer.
10. Divide into <ReactiveServings slug="basic-bread" servings={5} servingUnits={["loaf", "loaves"]} />.
11. Shape loaves and place in well-greased/sprayed pans. *(If using oil, use your finger to spread a thin layer of oil on the walls and bottom of the pan.)*
12. Cover with a dry towel and let rise until dough has risen double or approximately 2 inches above the pan. (**30-60 minutes**)
13. Bake in preheated oven at **325Â°F** for **30-35 minutes**.
14. Cool on cooling racks.
15. Butter tops while still warm.
